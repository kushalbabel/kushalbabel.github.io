I am a Senior Researcher at Category Labs. I received my PhD in Computer Science from Cornell Tech and IC3, where I was incredibly fortunate to be advised by Prof. Ari Juels. My research interests and experience span a variety of fields: Distributed Systems, Blockchains, Security, Applied Cryptography and Formal Verification.

I obtained my undergraduate degree in Computer Science & Engineering at IIT Bombay, where I worked on program verification with Prof. Supratik Chakraborty and NLP with Prof. Soumen Chakrabarti.

Prior to joining Cornell, I worked as a Quantitative Researcher and Low Latency C++ Engineer at a proprietary High Frequency Trading Firm in Mumbai and Singapore.
